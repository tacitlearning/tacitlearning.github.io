Your Instructor and Mentor

Dr. M Faysal Islam is a Graduate Faculty member and Doctoral Advisor at The George Washington University’s School of Engineering and Applied Science in Washington, DC. He holds a Ph.D. in Systems Engineering, where his dissertation developed a quantitative risk-analysis framework for cloud-computing reliability. He received an M.S. in Electrical Engineering and a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Complementing his academic pedigree, Dr. Islam brings extensive industry experience and a deep credential stack that spans project management (PMP, PMI-ACP, CSM), cybersecurity (CISSP, CEH), systems engineering (CSEP), enterprise architecture (TOGAF), and cloud platforms (AWS Certified Practitioner, Microsoft Azure AI).

Learning directly from an expert who bridges classroom knowledge by solving hands-on, real-world problems will give you the skills and confidence to accelerate your career.
